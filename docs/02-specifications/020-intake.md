# PART A — Product Specification

---

# 1. Purpose

The Intake module is the first business workflow within Practice365.

Its purpose is to capture prospective client enquiries before they become formal leads.

The Intake module standardizes how enquiries enter the system regardless of source.

Every intake submission may subsequently become a Lead through the Lead Management module.

The Intake module is intentionally lightweight and focuses only on collecting information.

No legal engagement or client relationship is established at this stage.

# 5. Business Requirements

## INTAKE-001

Every intake belongs to exactly one firm.

---

## INTAKE-002

Every intake receives a unique Intake Number generated by the system.

The numbering strategy is configurable at the firm level.

---

## INTAKE-003

Every intake records its source.

Examples include:

* Website
* Phone Call
* Email
* Walk-In
* Referral
* Advertisement
* Manual Entry

---

## INTAKE-004

An intake may be assigned to a staff member for review.

Assignment is optional.

---

## INTAKE-005

An intake progresses through a defined lifecycle.

Allowed statuses:

* New
* In Review
* Awaiting Response
* Qualified
* Rejected
* Converted

Status transitions are system controlled.

---

## INTAKE-006

One intake may be converted into exactly one Lead.

Once converted, the intake becomes read-only.

---

## INTAKE-007

Rejected intakes remain available for historical reporting.

Rejected intakes are never deleted.

---

## INTAKE-008

Supporting documents may be attached to an intake.

Files are stored using the platform's document storage strategy.

---

## INTAKE-009

Internal notes may be added to an intake.

Notes are visible only to firm users.

---

## INTAKE-010

Every significant intake event must be recorded by the Audit Log module.

Examples include:

* Intake Created
* Intake Assigned
* Intake Updated
* Intake Qualified
* Intake Rejected
* Intake Converted

# 6. User Stories

### Create Intake

As a staff member,

I want to create a new intake,

so a prospective client enquiry is captured.

---

### Review Intake

As an intake coordinator,

I want to review submitted intakes,

so I can determine the next action.

---

### Assign Intake

As a firm administrator,

I want to assign an intake,

so the correct staff member can handle it.

---

### Update Intake

As an assigned staff member,

I want to update intake information,

so the record remains accurate.

---

### Convert Intake

As an intake coordinator,

I want to convert a qualified intake into a lead,

so the lead management workflow can begin.

---

### Reject Intake

As an intake coordinator,

I want to reject unsuitable enquiries,

while preserving historical records.

# 12. Security Considerations

This module follows:

* 005 - Security Standards

The following security requirements are specific to the Intake module.

---

## Firm Isolation

Every intake belongs to exactly one firm.

Users may only access intake records belonging to their own firm.

Cross-firm access is prohibited.

---

## Assignment

Only users belonging to the same firm may be assigned to an intake.

Assignments across firms are prohibited.

---

## Status Changes

Only authorized users may change intake status.

All status transitions must follow the defined intake lifecycle.

Invalid state transitions must be rejected.

---

## Conversion

An intake may be converted into a Lead exactly once.

Once converted:

* The intake becomes read-only.
* Additional edits are prohibited.
* Duplicate conversions are prohibited.

---

## Attachments

Uploaded files must:

* Validate file type.
* Validate file size.
* Be stored in object storage.

MongoDB stores metadata only.

---

## Internal Notes

Intake notes are internal.

They must never be visible to prospective clients.

---

## Auditability

Every significant intake action must generate an Audit Log entry.

Examples include:

* Intake Created
* Intake Updated
* Intake Assigned
* Status Changed
* Attachment Uploaded
* Intake Converted
* Intake Rejected
