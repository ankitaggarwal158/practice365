# PART A — Product Specification

---

# 1. Purpose

The Notifications module delivers important system events to users through in-app and email notifications.

The module acts as a centralized notification engine used by other modules.

Notifications are informational only and never own business logic.

---

# 2. Scope

This module includes:

- In-App Notifications
- Email Notifications
- Read / Unread Status
- Notification Preferences
- Notification History
- Reminder Notifications
- Assignment Notifications
- Deadline Notifications

---

# 3. Out of Scope

This module does not include:

- SMS Notifications
- Push Notifications
- WhatsApp Notifications
- Marketing Emails
- Campaign Management

---

# 4. Dependencies

Business Module Dependencies

- 010 Authentication

Referenced By

# 5. Business Requirements

## NOTIFICATION-001

Every Notification belongs to exactly one Firm.

---

## NOTIFICATION-002

Every Notification belongs to exactly one User.

---

## NOTIFICATION-003

Notifications may originate from any business module.

---

## NOTIFICATION-004

Notifications support:

- Read
- Unread

---

## NOTIFICATION-005

Notifications may optionally trigger email delivery.

---

## NOTIFICATION-006

Notification history is retained for auditing.

---

## NOTIFICATION-007

Notifications are never permanently deleted.

Soft deletion is mandatory.

---

## NOTIFICATION-008

Every notification delivery attempt must be logged.

---

# 12. Security Considerations

This module follows:

- 005 - Security Standards

The following security requirements are specific to the Notifications module.

---

## Firm Isolation

Notifications belong to exactly one Firm.

Users may only access notifications belonging to themselves.

Cross-user access is prohibited.

---

## Read Status

Users may only mark their own notifications as read.

Unread counts are calculated server-side.

---

## Notification Integrity

Notifications are immutable after creation.

Only the following fields may change:

- isRead
- readAt
- deleted

Notification content cannot be modified.

---

## Email Delivery

Email delivery must respect user notification preferences.

Disabled notification categories must never generate email.

---

## Soft Delete

Notifications are never permanently deleted.

Deletion consists of:

- deleted = true

Historical delivery logs remain available.

---

## Auditability

Notification generation and delivery attempts should be recorded in the Audit Log.

---
